History

  • Metric System:

    • The Metric system dates back to the late 18th century during the french revolution. The French National Assembly commissioned a group of scientists to develop the system. This led to the creation of the "Decimal Metric System" in 1799, which later became known as the metric system. To this day over ninety-five percent of the world uses the metric system

  • Imperial:

    • The metric system traces back to ancient civilizations, including British Empire and the roman empire.The Imperial system used in the United States today was primarily taken from the British Empire. It was widely adopted across the British colonies, including the United States. It's still only primary used in the Untied States, Liberia, Myanmar today
